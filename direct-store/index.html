How It Works

What Is a Direct Store?

A custom online store built around your approved products, branding, and ordering rules. Your team orders what they need. We handle sourcing, decoration, and fulfillment.

01

Single Source

Safety gear, corporate apparel, and branded swag — all sourced, decorated, and fulfilled by RRI within your standards. No vendor juggling.

02

Approved Items Only

Only the gear you've selected appears in your store. No off-brand orders, no unauthorized products. Every item is pre-approved by your team.

03

Order Control

No off-program products. No logo changes. No vendor variation. All orders follow the same structure and all output matches previous orders.

04

Admin Control

Product selection is defined by program. Budgets can be controlled. User access is managed by role. Ordering stays aligned across teams.

05

In Stock & Ready

Core products are stocked and ready to move. When weather shifts or crews grow, you're not waiting on vendors.

06

Easy Reorders

If you've ordered it before, it's in the system. Get it again without the red tape. Simple, repeatable, reliable.
